About Us

Waterside Kitchens and Interiors has specialised in the design and installation of kitchens, bedrooms & bathrooms for nearly 35 years. After being based in the Thames Valley for the last 20 years, Waterside has now moved to Lopen in Somerset, and continues to install premium bespoke kitchens, bedrooms and bathrooms in the surrounding area.

Kitchens

Waterside Kitchens offers the complete kitchen service, from initial meeting to discuss ideas, through to design and installation. Whether it's a complete kitchen refit, replacement doors or new handles, Keith has over 35 years of knowledge to advise on the best styles and products to suit your kitchen requirements.

Bathrooms

Waterside Kitchens offers the complete bathroom service, from initial meeting to discuss ideas, through to design, planning and installation. Whether it's a complete bathroom, or just replacement components. A wide range available from Imperial, Eastbrooks and Ideal Standard.
