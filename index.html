About Us

Waterside Kitchens and Interiors has specialised in the design and installation of kitchens, bedrooms & bathrooms for nearly 35 years. After being based in the Thames Valley for the last 20 years, Waterside has now moved to Lopen in Somerset, and continues to install premium bespoke kitchens, bedrooms and bathrooms in the surrounding area.
